Overview

BKUUID is a drop-in replacement for NSUUID and is supported on iOS 5 and and higher. BKUUID is built around CFUUIDRef which has been available since iOS 2.0. BKUUID can be used to convert between CFUUIDRef and NSUUID objects as well as compare NSString, NSData, and NSUUID objects.
